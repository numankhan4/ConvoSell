# Security Incident Response Policy

## Objective
Provide a repeatable process to detect, contain, investigate, and notify incidents affecting personal data.

## Severity Levels
- Sev 1: Confirmed breach or active data exfiltration.
- Sev 2: High-risk unauthorized access or major service compromise.
- Sev 3: Suspicious activity with limited impact.

## Response Flow
1. Detect and triage.
2. Contain affected systems and rotate credentials.
3. Preserve logs and evidence.
4. Investigate scope and impacted data.
5. Notify internal stakeholders.
6. Notify merchants/regulators where required.
7. Remediate root cause and document lessons learned.

## Initial Notification Target
Notify impacted merchants without undue delay after confirming material impact.

## Evidence and Logging
Capture timeline, affected systems, data categories, and mitigation actions.

## Post-Incident Review
Run a postmortem within 5 business days with corrective action tracking.

## Action Required
Assign named on-call roles and escalation contacts.
