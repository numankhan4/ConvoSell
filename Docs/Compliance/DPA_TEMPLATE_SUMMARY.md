# Data Processing Addendum (DPA) Summary Template

## Parties
- Merchant: Data Controller
- App Provider: Data Processor

## Processing Scope
Processor handles customer/order/conversation data only to provide CRM, messaging, and Shopify integration services.

## Instructions
Processor processes personal data only on documented merchant instructions and service configuration.

## Confidentiality
Access to personal data is limited to authorized personnel under confidentiality obligations.

## Security Measures
- Access controls and role-based permissions.
- Encryption in transit.
- Logging of critical actions.
- Environment separation for development and production.

## Subprocessors
Processor may use subprocessors necessary for service operation and will maintain a subprocessor list.

## Data Subject Requests
Processor supports merchant handling of access, deletion, and correction requests.

## Retention and Deletion
Data is retained per policy and deleted/anonymized after service termination or request, subject to legal obligations.

## Incident Notification
Processor will notify merchants without undue delay after confirming a security incident affecting personal data.

## Cross-Border Transfers
Where applicable, processor applies lawful transfer mechanisms.

## Action Required
Convert this summary into signed legal terms (review by legal counsel) before claiming full contractual coverage.
